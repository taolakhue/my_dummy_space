Books that I've read

If you haven't paid attention to the trivia when you click into this section: If the cost of a ball and a bat is $1 and 10 cents, knowing that the bat costs $1 more than the ball. What does the ball costs? At the first glance, we all think that the ball costs 10 cents. But if we take time to think about it, we will figure out that the ball costs 5 cents and the bat costs $1 and 5 cents. Why do our brain make this very same mistake at first glance? We thought we are rational creatures but are we? My interest of studying about how your brain think starts there, that is why I paid attention to Artificial Intelligence especially Deep Learning and Machine Learning. In addition, I am also interested in Blockchain Technology that lets us manage our own data, our own money in the most secure and realiable ways.

The brain that changes itself

Your brain is not a machine that was born "hardwired" to do something. This book talks about neuroplasticity of the brain, that our brain is mutable, our brains can learn and change itself to be cured and treated if we put enough efforts.
